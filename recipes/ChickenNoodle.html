Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Chicken noodle soup is a comforting and hearty dish made with tender pieces of chicken,
soft egg noodles, and a flavorful broth. Often seasoned with herbs like thyme and
bay leaves, it also includes vegetables such as carrots, celery, and onions.
This classic soup is known for its soothing qualities, making it a go-to meal when
you're feeling udner the weather or simply in need of a warm, nourishing dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4(14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1(14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • 1/2 pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 1.2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
